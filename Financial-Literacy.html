💰 Financial Literacy For A Better Future

Empowering children and students with financial knowledge, entrepreneurship skills and smart money habits.

🏦 Why Financial Literacy Matters

Financial literacy helps children understand saving, budgeting, investing and responsible money management. Through Sampark and WisBees, we aim to make financial education engaging, practical and fun.

📚 What Students Will Learn

💵 Saving Money

Learn how to save and plan for future goals.

📊 Budgeting

Understand spending, saving and managing money.

🏦 Banking Basics

Learn how banks, UPI and digital payments work.

📈 Investing

Introduction to SIPs, Mutual Funds and wealth creation.

🚀 WisBees Business Carnival

The WisBees Business Carnival is a unique entrepreneurship and financial literacy program. Students participate in company formation, business simulations, banking challenges, investing activities and leadership exercises.

🎉 Special Event For Children In Orphanages

Learning Finance Through Fun

Sampark and WisBees are planning a special Financial Literacy & Entrepreneurship Event exclusively for children living in orphanages.

Through games, team activities, business simulations, money management exercises and leadership challenges, children will learn valuable life skills while having fun.
